chinese syllable¶

Syllable¶

  • List of Syllables in Pinyin The word syllable is a term referring to the units of a word, composed on an (optional) initial, a final, and a tone.

    The word "syllable" is 音节 (yīnjié) in Chinese.

    Most spoken syllables in Mandarin Chinese correspond to one written Chinese character.

    There are a total of 410 common pinyin syllables.

  • Mandarin Chinese Phonetics

  • chinese phonetics Chinese Characters, called “Hanzi”, are the writing symbols of the Chinese language. Pinyin is the Romanization of a phonetic notation for Chinese Characters. Each syllable is composed of three parts: initials, finals, and tones. In the Pinyin system there are 23 initials, 24 finals, 4 tones and a neutral tone.
